◈  DATA IS PROVENANCE

The best vintage pieces come with history.

The best data analysis tells the same story - where something came from, where it's going, and what it's worth right now. My Brookhaven internship trained me on large-scale datasets. Fashion just makes the subject matter infinitely more interesting.

◈  DESIGN IS ARGUMENT

A moodboard is not decoration.

It is a point of view, made visible. My Visual Arts minor taught me that every layout decision is a claim. My CS degree taught me to back claims with systems. The combination is uncommon, and it shows in the work.

◈  CODE IS CURATION

Writing a schema is an act of taste.

You decide what matters, what gets tracked, what gets remembered. For archival fashion, where every piece is one-of-one, that curatorial logic in the database is everything. I build systems that understand rarity.
